Egusi Recipe

  • Spice & steam the meat
  • put dryfish and allow it to boil
  • put your melon
  • add water to coverthe meat/fish/melon
  • put pepper salt crayfish
  • allow it to boil for 5 mins
  • put your ugwu leave or bitter leave
  • Allow it to cook for 2 mins under low heat

Obono Recipe

  • Spice & steam the meat
  • put dryfish and allow it to boil
  • put oil in a separate pot
  • allow to cook for 30s
  • slice onions and put inside
  • put obono and water inside
  • allow to stir for sometime
  • Then add your steamed protein
  • Add perewinkle, crayfish etc
  • Add vegetable and allo it to heat a bit
